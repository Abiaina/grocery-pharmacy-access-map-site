# Lake City Grocery & Pharmacy Access Map

A community advocacy tool documenting essential service closures in Seattle's Lake City neighborhood and mobilizing residents to restore access to groceries and medicine.

Live at [grocerypharmacyaccess.org](https://grocerypharmacyaccess.org)

---

## The Problem

Between 2023 and 2025, Lake City lost critical access to essential services:

- **3 pharmacies closed** — Walgreens (2023), Bartell Drugs (2023), and a third location
- **1 supermarket closed** — Fred Meyer (2025)
- **37% loss of pharmacy access** for the neighborhood
- **31,000 residents impacted**

This site exists to make that impact visible, give the community a voice, and push for real solutions.

---

## What the Site Does

### Interactive Impact Map
An interactive map of Lake City showing 15-minute walkability zones around open, closed, and limited-access grocery stores and pharmacies. Residents can see exactly how closures have affected their daily walking routes to essential services.

### Solutions Voting
Community members can vote on proposed solutions to the access crisis, including:
- Interim pharmacy services
- Expansion of remaining pharmacies
- Mobile pharmacy services
- A resident-centered task force for permanent solutions

### Petition
Residents can sign and share a petition calling for:
1. An interim pharmacy solution for Lake City
2. A permanent task force prioritizing existing infrastructure and union labor

### Printable Flyer
A downloadable flyer for community outreach and organizing.

---

## Community Demands

The site advocates for two core demands:
1. **An interim pharmacy solution** to restore immediate access for the 31,000 affected residents
2. **A resident-centered task force** to develop permanent solutions that prioritize existing infrastructure and union labor
