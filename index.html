Lake City Resilience Hub: Identify and Bridge Emerging Access Gaps

Problem: Between 2023 and 2025, Lake City lost three full-service pharmacies (Day, Evening, & Weekend coverage) and one supermarket, creating a critical food and pharmacy access gap in the neighborhood core. The remaining pharmacies are limited in capacity, have restrictive patient access, and one is not ADA accessible. Food access is constrained to farmers markets, small grocers, and corner stores.

Solution: Expand Pharmacy Access Now, supported by a Task Force and targeted policy changes to ensure residents maintain consistent access to medications and groceries.

Action: Sign the petition and vote on short and long term solutions to strengthen Lake City's essential service infrastructure.
