Summary

Problem:

We have lost 3 pharmacies and 1 supermarket, creating a food & pharmacy access gap in the core of Lake City.

Solution:

We need an Interim Pharmacy, Task Force and Policy Changes.

Interim Pharmacy

Stand up a neighborhood pharmacy that accepts Medicare Part D, Apple Health (Medicaid) and commercial insurance plans.

Task Force

  • • Community-centered task force
  • • Identify permanent grocery + pharmacy model (co-op, municipal or conventional grocer)
  • • prioritize re-use of existing infrastructure, access, and union labor

Policy Changes

  • • Ban restrictive covenants on former grocery/pharmacy sites; expedite permits for re-tenanting.
  • • Adopt time-based pharmacy access standards (minutes, not just miles) to reflect Lake City's topography.
  • • Create a pharmacy "Any Willing Provider" state law (plans/Pharmacy Benefit Managers must offer standard terms); ensure Qualified Health Plan (QHP) network adequacy explicitly includes pharmacies.
